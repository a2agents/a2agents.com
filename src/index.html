About

I've spent my career at the intersection of human potential and technological innovation. After leading teams through multiple digital transformations, I realized something crucial was missing from how we approach AI adoption.

Too many organizations treat AI as a purely technical challenge, forgetting that successful transformation requires people who feel empowered, not replaced. That's why I founded Agent to Agents.

My approach combines executive coaching expertise with deep AI knowledge, helping leaders navigate both the human and technological aspects of change. I believe the future belongs to those who can bridge these worlds.

Whether I'm coaching a CEO on leadership presence or helping a team implement AI workflows, my goal is the same: create organizations where human creativity and AI capability amplify each other.
